How our Escrow Works

Trade Creation
The buyer initiates a new trade and receives a secure tracking password along with a unique link to share with the other party.

Access for the Seller
The seller clicks the link and is issued their own private access password.

Payment & Delivery
The buyer sends the agreed payment. The seller delivers the specified product or service. Once received and verified, the buyer finalizes the transaction. The seller can then withdraw the funds.

Automatic Release
If the buyer does not manually release the funds within the set finalization window, the system will automatically release the funds to the seller.

Status Updates
The seller is encouraged to update their site or provide confirmation after shipping the product, ensuring transparency throughout the process.

Dispute Resolution
If the seller fails to deliver as agreed, the buyer can open a dispute. A secure chat is initiated between the buyer, seller, and a platform moderator to resolve the issue.
